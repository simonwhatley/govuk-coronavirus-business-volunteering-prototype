Offer your products to an NHS Regional Coordination Centre

If you’re offering less than 1,000 items of a personal protective equipment product, you should offer them to an NHS Regional Coordination Centre.

How to offer your products

Email the centre giving details of the products you’re offering, including whether they meet the technical specifications.

You can email any centre, but you will have to transport the products yourself or arrange for them to be delivered.

You will be contacted as soon as possible if your products are needed.
